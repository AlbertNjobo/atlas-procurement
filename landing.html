FAQ
Frequently asked questions

Helping you understand how Atlas delivers security, compliance, and smarter procurement automation.

Do I need technical skills to set this up? ▼

No. Atlas is designed for procurement professionals, not engineers. Describe what you need in plain language and the AI handles the rest.

What can your AI agents automate? ▼

Everything from supplier research and price negotiation to PO creation, invoice processing, and 3-way matching. Atlas handles the full procure-to-pay cycle.

How secure is my data? ▼

Atlas uses Firebase Firestore with role-based security rules. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. You maintain full control over access permissions.

Can I test agents before going live? ▼

Yes. All plans include sandbox mode where you can test agent workflows without affecting production data.

What tools can I integrate with? ▼

Atlas integrates with Slack, Google Workspace, CRMs, and any tool with an API. The agent marketplace also offers pre-built integrations.
